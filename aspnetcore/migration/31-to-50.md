<span data-ttu-id="6c303-120">Con el comportamiento anterior en ASP.NET Core 5.0.0 o versiones anteriores, las rutas con menor prioridad, como `{*slug}` , se comparan antes que las rutas con mayor precedencia, como `/customer/{id}` .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6c303-120">With the prior behavior in ASP.NET Core 5.0.0 or earlier, routes with lower precedence, such as `{*slug}`, are matched before routes with higher precedence, such as `/customer/{id}`.</span></span>
